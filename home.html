WHAT IS INFORMATION SYSTEM AND WHY IS IT
IMPORTANT:

An information system (IS) is a networked collection of parts that are used to gather, store, process, and send digital and analogue data. Fundamentally, it is a collection of technology, software, data, personnel, and procedures that collaborate to convert unprocessed input into meaningful knowledge. Numerous company goals, including enhanced customer service and better productivity, are supported by an IS. Benefits of IS include

  • Efficient operations
  • Data Management
  • Decision-Making support
  • Competitive Advantage
  • Customer relationship management
  • Global Connectivity

THE PURPOSE OF THIS TOOLKIt AND ITS SIGNIFICANCE:

The goal of the toolkit is to improve business professionals' understanding of IT. It's crucial since it raises information literacy levels, which facilitate businesspeople's efficient use of technology.

THE OBJECTIVE:

The project's goal is to give students hands-on experience with the importance of information systems in business, with a focus on the convergence of software development and computer science.

EDUCATIONAL PURPOSE:
-Close the gap between theoretical understanding and practical applications in education.
-Give students the necessary tools to handle real-world business problems.
- Encourage the capacity to make decisions based on data.
-Improve knowledge of how computer science, software development, and information systems are related.
